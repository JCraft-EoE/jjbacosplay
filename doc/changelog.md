* **added horse skins**

## Tiered Cosplay
* to make cosplay easier accessible, you can now craft your cosplay piece from **any** vanilla armor
* this will lose the enchantments on the used item just like before, BUT
* you can now upgrade your cosplay to the next tier, using an anvil, and that WILL retain and even combine the enchantments
* armor icons are now given a little subicon to indicate their tier
## Horse Skins
* if you name your horse like one of the show, it will change its skin to match the show
